What Is FIRST?

FIRST stands for: For Inspiration and Recognition of Science and Technology.Their mission is to inspire young people to explore careers in science, technology and engineering.

By running robotics competitions for school-age students at the elementary, junior high school and senior high school level, they are able to inspire students to pursue their dreams.
